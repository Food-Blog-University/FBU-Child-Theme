**** Accordion Drop Down ****

The accordion drop down feature should be carefully created because it can easily be messed up if you accidentally delete a div container or missing something.  To begin an accordion, you need to create a box or container for each panel to go in, like this:


<div class="accordion">
	This is where your accordion panels go
</div>

To see the live demo:
http://demos.styledthemes.com/pure-simple/accordion/





*** This is one panel ***

<h5 class="panel panel-red">YOUR TITLE</h5>
<div class="panel panel-red ">
<p>YOUR CONTENT HERE!</p>
</div>

Important: Copy your title in the Your Title Field and its content to its corresponding "p" tag. 



*************** Adding Colour *****************
You can also colourize your accordion by adding a colour class to the <h5> and <div> container like this: 

<h5 class="panel panel-green">
<div class="panel panel-green">


You get the following colours:

blue
darkblue
lightblue
orange
green
red
brown
custom
