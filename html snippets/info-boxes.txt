**** Info Boxes ****

Info boxes are simple containers for messages and other content. You add a class to the container your content is in, such as a <p> or <div>

Code snippets: 

<p class="info-box">Duis eget neque molestie, imperdiet mi eget, Quisque dolor mi, condimentum eu lectus quis, auctor pellentesque ligula. Ut id leo imperdiet, rhoncus justo malesuada, congue sapien.</p>


<div class="info-box">Duis eget neque molestie, imperdiet mi eget, Quisque dolor mi, condimentum eu lectus quis, auctor pellentesque ligula. Ut id leo imperdiet, rhoncus justo malesuada, congue sapien.</div>

You get the following colours:

Default is: info-box
Grey: info-box-grey
Rust: info-box-rust
Tan: info-box-tan

For a grey info box, it's done like this:

<p class="info-box-grey">Duis eget neque molestie, imperdiet mi eget, Quisque dolor mi, condimentum eu lectus quis, auctor pellentesque ligula. Ut id leo imperdiet, rhoncus justo malesuada, congue sapien.</p>
