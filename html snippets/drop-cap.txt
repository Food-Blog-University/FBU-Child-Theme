**** Drop Cap ****

A drop cap is simply an oversized letter or number that starts off with the first letter of a word in your first paragraph. This is done by adding this at the beginning:

<span class="dropcap">A</span>

* Replace the "A" part with the first letter of your word like this sample:

<p><span class="dropcap">M</span>oram ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Sed blandit nulla at orci aliquet, vitae tincidunt lacus pretium. Ut hendrerit fermentum lorem fringilla commodo.</p>
