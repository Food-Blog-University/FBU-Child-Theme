**** Text Inputs with Append or Prepend ****


PREPEND: This allows you to add a label or icon, before the field:

<div class="input-group">
<div class="input-group-addon">
<i class="fa fa-user"></i>
</div>
<p><input class="form-control" type="text" placeholder="Username"></p>
</div>

APPEND: This allows you to add a label or icon, before the field:

<div class="input-group">
<p><input class="form-control" type="text" placeholder="Username"></p>
<div class="input-group-addon">
<i class="fa fa-user"></i>
</div>
</div>
