**** Blockquotes ****

For a quote with a quote icon to the left and a cite included:

<blockquote class="quotes"><p>Nullam ac tristique ligula. Quisque sit amet ex dictum risus sodales convallis. Sed tincidunt iaculis viverra. Etiam sodales dignissim ante, a hendrerit eros vulputate quis.<cite>Parker Lorem</cite></p>
</blockquote>

This will also work without adding the class="quotes" to it:

<blockquote><p>Nullam ac tristique ligula. Quisque sit amet ex dictum risus sodales convallis. Sed tincidunt iaculis viverra. Etiam sodales dignissim ante, a hendrerit eros vulputate quis.<cite>Parker Lorem</cite></p>
</blockquote>


For a quote style using a vertical green bar:

<blockquote class="bar green"><p>Nullam ac tristique ligula. Quisque sit amet ex dictum risus sodales convallis. Sed tincidunt iaculis viverra. Etiam sodales dignissim ante, a hendrerit eros vulputate quis.<cite>Parker Lorem</cite></p>
</blockquote>


Available Colours for the Bar Style:

green
grey
rust
Tan


For a blockquote that is off to the side, you add a class like: pull-left 
If you want it to the right, then add a class like: pull-right


<blockquote class="pull-left"><p>In lectus lectus, fringilla et orci in, porta sollicitudin leo. Vestibulum ullamcorper ex eros, ut eleifend nisl aliquet id. Morbi at egestas felis. Duis sit amet blandit augue.&nbsp;</p>
</blockquote>
