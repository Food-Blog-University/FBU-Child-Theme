**** Widgetkit Slide Captions ****

How you style your captions will be up to you and based on the subject matter in your photos. 
Photos will require different caption positions, whether from the left or right, and colour
of your text. 

Font Sizing and other styles can be applied to these snippets such as italic text, font weight, etc.
For sizing, it's best to do this in a child theme or CSS Editor plugin because you need to maintain 
adjusted sizing for mobile devices. Please see the main theme's style.css for the widgetkit slideshow css.
